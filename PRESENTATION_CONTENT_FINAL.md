## Slide 4: Customer Behavior Analytics
**Title:** Customer Behavior Analytics & Data-Driven Insights

**1. Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** — Traffic & Conversion Tracking
   - Booking conversion rate: 92% | Event completion: 88%
   - Tracks page views, session duration, acquisition channels
   - Optimizes marketing spend across organic, direct & referral

**2. Mixpanel** — Event Tracking & Funnel Analysis
   - Discovery → Booking: 92% | Search → View: 87% | View → Booking: 85%
   - Tracks Search, Booking, Reviews, Sign-Up & 4 more events
   - Identifies drop-off points to optimize user flows

**3. PostHog** — Product Analytics & Feature Adoption
   - AR Menu: 45% | Waitlist: 62% | Pre-orders: 58% adoption
   - User segments: High-value (3+ bookings/mo), Casual, Event-focused
   - A/B testing + session recordings for UX insights

**4. Vercel Analytics** — Web Performance & Core Web Vitals
   - LCP <2.5s | FID <100ms | CLS <0.1
   - Real-time alerts for performance degradation
   - Ensures fast experience → higher conversion
---

### Customer Behavior Analytics - Key Insights

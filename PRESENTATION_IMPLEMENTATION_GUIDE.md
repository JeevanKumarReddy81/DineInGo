# DineInGo Presentation - Implementation Guide

## Visual Design Recommendations

### Brand Colors
- **Primary Color:** #FF6B35 (Vibrant Orange - Energy & Appetite)
- **Secondary Color:** #004E89 (Deep Blue - Trust & Professionalism)
- **Accent Color:** #F7B801 (Gold - Premium & Success)
- **Background:** #FFFFFF (White) or #F5F5F5 (Light Gray)
- **Text:** #333333 (Dark Gray) or #000000 (Black)

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, Sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter, or Poppins)
- **Body Text:** Regular, Sans-serif (Open Sans, Roboto, or Lato)
- **Font Sizes:**
  - Title: 54-60pt
  - Subtitle: 32-36pt
  - Heading: 28-32pt
  - Body: 18-24pt
  - Small text: 14-16pt

### Design Elements
- Use restaurant and food imagery
- Include customer avatars and testimonials
- Add charts and infographics for data
- Use icons for visual interest
- Include QR codes for engagement
- Add subtle animations in digital presentation

## Presentation Delivery Tips

### Before the Presentation
1. **Practice Thoroughly**
   - Rehearse 3-4 times
   - Time each section
   - Practice transitions
   - Prepare for Q&A

2. **Technical Setup**
   - Test projector/screen
   - Check audio/video
   - Verify internet connection
   - Have backup on USB

3. **Audience Preparation**
   - Send agenda in advance
   - Provide handouts
   - Set expectations
   - Prepare Q&A materials

### During the Presentation
1. **Opening (1 minute)**
   - Welcome audience
   - Introduce yourself
   - State presentation objective
   - Outline agenda

2. **Problem Section (3 minutes)**
   - Tell a compelling story
   - Use real customer quotes
   - Show market data
   - Build urgency

3. **Solution Section (4 minutes)**
   - Demonstrate product
   - Show real screenshots
   - Highlight innovations
   - Explain benefits

4. **Validation Section (5 minutes)**
   - Share customer feedback
   - Show analytics data
   - Present growth metrics
   - Build credibility

5. **Business Section (4 minutes)**
   - Explain revenue model
   - Show financial projections
   - Highlight competitive advantage
   - Present growth opportunity

6. **Closing (2 minutes)**
   - Summarize key points
   - Reiterate value proposition
   - Call to action
   - Thank audience

### Q&A Session (5-10 minutes)
1. **Listen Carefully**
   - Let questioner finish
   - Clarify if needed
   - Take notes

2. **Answer Effectively**
   - Be concise
   - Use data to support
   - Admit if you don't know
   - Offer to follow up

3. **Common Questions**
   - "How is this different from competitors?"
   - "What's your go-to-market strategy?"
   - "What are your unit economics?"
   - "How will you acquire customers?"
   - "What's your funding ask?"

## 🛡️ Security Disclosure Policy

### Responsible Disclosure
DineInGo follows a **responsible disclosure policy** for security vulnerabilities. We appreciate security researchers and encourage them to report vulnerabilities responsibly.

### Reporting Process
1. **Discovery:** Identify a potential security vulnerability
2. **Report:** Send details to security@dineingo.com
3. **Acknowledgment:** We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
4. **Investigation:** Our team will investigate and assess severity
5. **Fix:** We will work on a fix based on severity level
6. **Disclosure:** We will coordinate disclosure timeline with you

### What NOT to Do
- ❌ Do not publicly disclose the vulnerability before we fix it
- ❌ Do not access or modify user data
- ❌ Do not perform denial-of-service attacks
- ❌ Do not access systems beyond the scope of testing
- ❌ Do not share vulnerability details with third parties

### What We Will Do
- ✅ Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
- ✅ Provide regular updates on fix progress
- ✅ Credit you in our security advisory (if desired)
- ✅ Offer bug bounty rewards (for critical vulnerabilities)
- ✅ Coordinate responsible disclosure timeline

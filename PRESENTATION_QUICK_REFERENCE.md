## 🎤 PRESENTATION TIPS

1. **Tell a Story** - Don't just present data, tell a compelling narrative
2. **Use Visuals** - Show, don't tell. Use screenshots and charts
3. **Know Your Numbers** - Be prepared to discuss metrics in detail
4. **Practice Delivery** - Rehearse multiple times to build confidence
5. **Engage Audience** - Ask questions, encourage participation
6. **Handle Q&A** - Listen carefully, answer concisely, admit if unsure
7. **Follow Time** - Stick to 15-20 minutes for main presentation
8. **End Strong** - Summarize key points and call to action

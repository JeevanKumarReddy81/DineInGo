## PRESENTATION NOTES

### Design Guidelines:
- Use DineInGo brand colors (primary: #FF6B35, secondary: #004E89)
- Include high-quality restaurant and event imagery
- Use charts and infographics for data visualization
- Keep text minimal, use bullet points
- Include customer testimonials with photos
- Use icons for visual interest

### Presentation Flow:
1. Start with problem (Slide 2)
2. Show research & personas (Slide 3)
3. Present solution (Slides 4-5)
4. Demonstrate with real data (Slides 6-8)
5. Show competitive advantage (Slide 9)
6. End with business model & growth (Slide 10)
7. Call to action (Slide 11)

### Timing:
- Total presentation: 15-20 minutes
- Slide 1: 1 minute (introduction)
- Slides 2-3: 3 minutes (problem & research)
- Slides 4-5: 4 minutes (solution & demo)
- Slides 6-8: 5 minutes (feedback & analytics)
- Slides 9-10: 4 minutes (competitive advantage & business model)
- Slide 11: 2 minutes (call to action)
- Q&A: 5-10 minutes

### Key Talking Points:
- Emphasize real-time synchronization as key differentiator
- Highlight strong growth metrics (25% MoM)
- Show customer satisfaction (4.6/5.0 rating)
- Demonstrate market opportunity (₹150B+ global market)
- Present clear path to profitability
- Show strong unit economics (LTV:CAC = 56.7:1)
